About Us

Who we are ?

The pharmaceutical marketplace is changing dramatically, with high implications for the industry as a whole. The number of SKUs per distributor is increasing with the changed market practices. The demand has become so unique in nature that a traditional distributor is finding it difficult coping up with the change of pace in the industry. We at OP Pharma Distributor Pvt Ltd, are a service-oriented team of caring people who feel duty-bound for best possible services to our customers (retailers, hospital pharmacies etc) which can be a saviour for someone.

What we do?

OP Pharma supplies branded, generic and over-the-counter (OTC) pharmaceuticals to more than 600 customers spanning independent retail pharmacies, retail chains and institutional providers such as hospitals, nursing homes & dispensaries. We guarantee the continuous availability of all kinds of medicines through organized yet onerous procedures, inventory control and product handling procedures.
